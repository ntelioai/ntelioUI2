Navigation Widgets

BreadCrumbs, Sidebar, and Dock — content-level navigation components.

BreadCrumbs

Arrow-shaped breadcrumb trail with slide-in animation and home icon.

Sidebar

Categorical sub-navigation with active state tracking. Click items to see events.

Click a sidebar item to navigate.

Dock — vertical

macOS-style application dock with fisheye magnification. Move the cursor along the dock to see the icon under it grow while its neighbours scale less the further they sit from the pointer. Hover an item to reveal its tooltip. Click an action to mark it active — the small dot pokes out the opposite edge from the tooltip.

Hover the dock to magnify
Click a dock item to see the select event…

Dock — horizontal

Same widget, orientation: 'horizontal'. Magnification grows upward; tooltips appear above.
